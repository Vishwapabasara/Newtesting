Amba Travels

AmbaTravels provides a wide array of tour packages in Sri Lanka. Travel in luxury with your own private vehicle, trek by foot or even fly around our fascinating island. We cater to adventurous travelers with our ‘Sri Lanka by Cycle’. For those who want to feel pampered our ‘Ayurvedic Journey’ or spa treatments are on hand while heritage and culture are a-plenty on the ‘Southern Riveria’ tour. Don’t miss out on frolicking with the dolphins or re-walking in the steps of Rama, Sita, and Ravana – book now with Amba Travels.
